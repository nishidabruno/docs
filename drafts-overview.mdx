You can create your first draft in two ways:

1. By clicking on **1**.

### Navigating the canvas

- **Click and hold** the middle mouse button to move the canvas horizontally and vertically.
- **Scroll up or down** to move the canvas horizontally.
- **Hold Shift** and scroll up or down to move the canvas vertically.
- **Hold Ctrl** and scroll up or down to zoom in or out.
